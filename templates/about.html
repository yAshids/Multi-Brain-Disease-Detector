About Us

AI for better brain health: Early detection, smarter care.

Who We Are

We're a team of developers and healthcare researchers passionate about using artificial intelligence to improve early diagnosis of brain-related diseases. Our solution supports detection of Alzheimer's, strokes, and brain tumors through medical imaging.

What We Do

We integrate deep learning models with a clean interface that allows clinicians and users to upload scans, receive predictions, and generate reports-all in one place. Our goal is to make detection accessible and reliable.

Our Vision

We envision a healthcare system where no patient has to wait too long for a diagnosis. Our AI platform bridges the gap between medical professionals and advanced diagnostics, especially in underserved regions.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast and accurate AI-powered results
  • Supports multiple brain diseases
  • Downloadable, doctor-friendly PDF reports
  • Free to use and built with care
